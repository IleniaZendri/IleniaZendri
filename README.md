### 👋 About me
 
#### Derivatives Middle Office Analyst | Commodity & Energy Markets | Market Risk
 
Hello! 👋 I'm Ilenia, a Business Analyst working at the crossroads of **financial markets, energy trading and risk management**.

Business Analyst with experience in financial markets and energy trading, combining a strong
quantitative background with hands-on exposure to risk management and trading operations.
MSc in Banking & Finance and BSc in Computer Science, bringing strong IT skills alongside a solid
financial foundation. FRM Part I candidate and holder of Swiss certifications in Commodity Trading & Risk
Analysis.

Deeply passionate about Derivatives and Middle and Front Office activities, and actively seeking to build a career in
this field.
